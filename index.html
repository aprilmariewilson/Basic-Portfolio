About Me

My whole life I have lived by the philosiphy that what you put into life is what you will get out. I am an adventurous, enthusiastic person who loves learning and pushing myself to the limits. I endeavor to excel with my competetive spirit. I feel I am at my best under pressure and that I find clarity in chaos.I have a natural curiosity that drives my actions. I want to know how things work and why. I want to learn how to do everything. I see a problem and my drive makes gives me a need to come up with a solution.

In my spare time I love the ocean and the sun. I love to travel and experience life in different cultures. I enjoy walking a mile in other peoples' shoes, if you will. I want to eat what the locals eat, go where they go and do what they do. I want to explore and i truly get lost in the moment.
